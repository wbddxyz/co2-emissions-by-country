Main Causes of CO₂ Pollution

Home Reduce Footprint Causes of CO₂ Pollution Top Emitters

1. Fossil Fuels

Burning coal, oil, and natural gas for energy and transportation is the largest source of CO₂ emissions worldwide.

2. Deforestation

Clearing forests reduces the number of trees that can absorb CO₂, increasing atmospheric CO₂ levels.

3. Industrial Processes

Industries like cement and steel production release significant CO₂ during manufacturing.

4. Agriculture

Agricultural activities produce CO₂ and other greenhouse gases, especially through livestock farming and rice production.
